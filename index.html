Healthcare AI Knowledge Systems

KalpaneAI applies Retrieval-Augmented Generation to trusted medical manuals and research papers, converting authoritative references into clear, reliable, and instantly accessible answers.

What We Solve

Healthcare professionals face information overload, fragmented access to knowledge, cognitive burden, and the risk of outdated references. KalpaneAI is designed to reduce that friction.

  • Transforms trusted medical manuals into real-time, actionable insights
  • Improves access to clinical knowledge under time pressure
  • Supports safer and more informed healthcare decision-making
  • Expandable to local hospital and country-specific guidelines

How It Works

The solution uses smart retrieval to pull relevant content from trusted sources and combines it with generative AI to produce concise, evidence-backed responses.

  • Smart retrieval from trusted medical references such as the Merck Manual
  • AI-powered clarity grounded in authoritative sources
  • Can extend to NHS, Dutch, or hospital-specific guidelines
  • Designed for domain-focused healthcare knowledge workflows

KRAI Index — Responsible AI Evaluation
