What We Build

KalpaneAI is evolving across two complementary tracks: applying AI to trusted healthcare knowledge, and helping organisations evaluate and govern generative AI systems in line with Dutch and EU expectations.

Healthcare AI Knowledge Systems

KalpaneAI applies Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to trusted medical manuals and research papers, transforming authoritative references into clear, reliable, and instantly accessible answers for healthcare professionals.

  • Trusted medical knowledge transformed into actionable insights
  • Smart retrieval from sources such as the Merck Manual
  • AI-powered clarity with concise, evidence-backed responses
  • Scalable from one medical manual to multiple sources and local guidelines

Responsible AI Governance

KalpaneAI develops evaluation approaches for market-ready LLMs, helping organisations understand how models align with the EU AI Act, GDPR, Dutch regulatory expectations, and AP guidance.

  • Evaluation of market AI solutions and open-weight models
  • Privacy, governance, and data protection alignment
  • Robustness, hallucination control, and reliability assessment
  • Bias, transparency, and human-in-the-loop oversight

Responsible AI Evaluation Themes

Our Responsible AI work is shaped around practical dimensions that matter for enterprise deployment, regulatory readiness, and trust in Generative AI systems.

Market AI Solutions Foundation models, open weights, rapid deployment, enterprise adoption
Privacy & Governance GDPR principles, access control, data compliance, traceability
Robustness Validation layers, guardrails, reliability, fallback mechanisms
Fairness & Oversight Bias testing, non-discrimination, transparency, human-in-the-loop
