Healthcare AI Knowledge Systems

KalpaneAI applies Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to trusted medical manuals and research papers, converting authoritative references into clear, reliable, and instantly accessible answers.

What We Solve

Healthcare professionals face information overload, fragmented access to knowledge, cognitive burden, and the risk of outdated references. KalpaneAI is designed to reduce that friction.

  • Transforms trusted medical manuals into real-time, actionable insights
  • Improves access to clinical knowledge under time pressure
  • Supports safer and more informed healthcare decision-making
  • Can scale from one source to multiple medical references and local guidelines

How It Works

The solution uses RAG to retrieve the most relevant content from trusted sources and combines it with generative AI to produce concise, evidence-backed responses.

  • Smart retrieval from sources such as the Merck Manual
  • AI-powered clarity grounded in authoritative references
  • Expandable to country-specific and hospital-specific guidelines
  • Designed for domain-focused healthcare knowledge workflows

KRAI Index — Responsible AI Evaluation

Vision
